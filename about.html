About Xenex

We Provide top fiberglass and steel fabrication services.

At Xenex, we specialize in the installation, maintenance, and management of glass reinforced piping (GRP, GRE, FRP) and steel fabrication works. Our expertise spans across a wide range of industrial sectors, including oil and gas, power plants, desalination plants, chemical plants, and vessel projects.

We pride ourselves on our unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. From concept to completion, we ensure that every project is executed with precision and professionalism, adhering to international standards and best practices.

At Xenex, we are driven by a passion for excellence and a relentless pursuit of innovation. Our goal is to exceed expectations, provide sustainable solutions, and contribute to the advancement of industries worldwide.

Join us in shaping the future of fiberglass and steel fabrication. Experience the Xenex difference today.
